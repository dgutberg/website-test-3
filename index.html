What is ringette?



Ringette is a team sport that is played on ice. It is played mainly by girls in Canada.

It is a safe, non-contact, fast and competitive sport.




















There are different levels you can play based on you skill level and intensity. There are teams across Canada for every age group. There is a team for each province.





















You use a long stick and pass a blue rubber ring around the ice. The stick is usually made out of wood but can also be made out of other materials if they are approved by Ringette Canada. The ring is made out of blue rubber, It is made out of blue rubber because that material lasts a long time and does not wareout easily. The goal is to score more goals than the other team. At the end of the game, the team with the most goals wins.
